ShopMetrics E-Commerce Customer Behaviour EDA
**Domain:** E-Commerce Analytics

A full customer behaviour analysis for ShopMetrics with 2,000 customers and 8,500+ orders across two years. Part of a three-project e-commerce series alongside the SQL analysis and Power BI dashboard.

**12 Analysis Sections:**
- Monthly revenue trends with MoM growth tracking
- Category and product performance with margin analysis
- Pareto / revenue concentration — Lorenz curve
- Purchase frequency and basket size distribution
- 12-cohort retention heatmap tracking return rates month by month
- Full RFM segmentation classifying customers into 8 behavioural groups
- Regional and payment method performance
- Seasonal buying patterns — day of week and monthly heatmap
- Executive dashboard — 10 KPI cards in dark theme
- Key findings and top 5 recommendations

**Key findings:** Top 20% of customers drive 75% of revenue · Q4 accounts for 30 to 35% of annual revenue · Asia Pacific shows highest AOV despite being third largest region by order volume

**Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn · Jupyter Notebook
